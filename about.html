About Me
Currently studying and working out of New Hampshire, my knowledge and understanding of graphic design goes back to my studies in high school. Finding a passion for this field here, I have continued to develop my design skills through pursing a BA in Graphic Design, as well as taking small design jobs within my local area.

Experience
Considering my work, my areas of expertise revolve around layout design, UI/UX design, typographic design, and web development. Additionally, my works are all created through utilizing the Adobe Creative Suite, which has enhanced my proficiencies with these products.

Goals
My career goals are currently to create innovative design solutions for individuals and small businesses, and to continue to enhance my graphic design skills.
